Helping The Orphans

Allah's Messenger (ﷺ) said, "I and the one who looks after an orphan will be like this in Paradise," showing his middle and index fingers and separating them.
The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "One who strives to help the widows and the poor is like the one who fights in the way of Allah." [Al-Bukhari]
Al-Risalah has opened a questionnaire about organising help for the orphans nad widows who need help. Firstly, those who can contribute, will spend a monthyl sum to support a family in need of help. Secondly, putting a designated donations box, managed by three people outside of our oganisation and its funds go to the needy families. We would appreciate any suggestions or comments.

A RISALAH AND AL NOOR

Al-Noor organisation and Al-Risalah organisation have come together to announce that clothes and shoes donations are accepted to help our brothers and sisters of all ages and all kinds of clothes. A specific day will be decided later to see what type of clothes families need. We ask those who donate to only give clothes that are clean and wearable. Clothes will be accepted through Al Noor or Al Risalah. Contact us through our facebook page or through email.
